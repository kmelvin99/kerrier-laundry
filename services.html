Our Services

Pickup, wash, fold, and delivery — we handle it all so you don't have to.

Residential Services

For individuals and families in Franklin, KY and the surrounding area.

🧺

Pickup & Delivery

We come to you. Schedule a pickup time and we'll grab your laundry right from your door — porch drop or hand-off, whatever works best for you.

👕

Wash & Fold

Every load is washed with care using quality detergents like Gain, Tide, and Arm & Hammer. Colors and whites are sorted appropriately and everything comes back neatly folded.

⚡

24-Hour Turnaround

We deliver your clean laundry within 24 hours of pickup. Need a specific drop-off time? Just let us know and we'll work around your schedule.

🪝

Hang & Return

Need items hung instead of folded? We've got you covered. Just request it when booking — hangers are provided at a small additional fee.

🚀

Same-Day Service

In a pinch? We offer same-day delivery for those moments when you just can't wait. An additional fee applies — contact us to check availability.

🔄

Recurring Service

Set up a regular schedule and save at least 15% on every order. Perfect for busy households who want clean laundry without ever thinking about it.

Airbnb & Short-Term Rental Linens
