About Us

Who We Are

Our names are Kelly Kerr and Maranda Cox (soon to be Kerr). We have three children, two dogs (Indoor), one cat (outdoor), two bunnies (escapes often outside), and one turtle. We know a thing or two about laundry with ALL these kids and such.

Why We Started Kerrier Laundry Co.

We started this business because we feel that we cannot be the only ones trying to get their time back from all the laundry that has to be done between everyone in the house. We want to be able to help anyone from people with busy work schedules who want to relax to families who just need the time to spend with each other rather than on laundry. On average for our family for example we spend an average 6-8 hours a week washing, drying, and folding clothes, and linens.

Our Promise To You

Every bag is treated like our own, we are particular and like to make sure everything is looking good, smelling good, and can be put away easily! We strive for convienence and also strive for a 24-48 hour turn around time. We always try to put people first and work with everyone on a schedule that works for them.
