About Us

Who We Are

Our names are Kelly Kerr and Maranda Cox (soon to be Kerr). We have three children, two dogs (Indoor), one cat (outdoor), two bunnies (escapes often outside), and one turtle. We know a thing or two about laundry with ALL these kids and such.

Why We Started Kerrier Laundry Co.

We started this business because we feel that we cannot be the only ones trying to get their time back from all the laundry that has to be done between everyone in the house. Whether you have a busy schedule and have no time for laundry or you just want more time with your own family, we want to help you! We know that between washing, drying, and folding it can take up to 6-8 hours per week from yourselves and your family. We want to give that time back to you.

Our Promise To You

Every bag is treated like our own, we want to make sure everything looks good, smells good, and can easily be put away. We strive for convenience as well as a 24-48 hour turn around time. We always try to put people first, if you don't see a time or option that works for you, reach out to us so we can work something out!
